## Mechanical Ventilation Controversy

Previously, standard practice was to give SUP to anyone on mechanical ventilation without parenteral feeding.

Most recent guidelines had **dropped** this recommendation. However, the **REVISE trial** indicated decreased GI bleed with prophylaxis in mechanically ventilated patients.

> REVISE Trial: *Stress ulcer prophylaxis during invasive mechanical ventilation.* NEJM 2024.

!!! warning "Clear as mud, right?"
    The mechanical ventilation indication remains debated. Follow your institution's protocol.
