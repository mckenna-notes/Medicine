# ICP

*Intracranial Pressure*

---

## Cerebral Edema

- No evidence that hypertonic solution helps lower ICP in severe **metabolic encephalopathy**

There are four main pathologies for ICP. Differentiating between the two most common causes has an important clinical distinction regarding corticosteroid use:

| Type | Cause | Corticosteroids |
|---|---|---|
| **Vasogenic (inflammatory)** | Brain tumors, inflammatory conditions involving BBB disruption | ✅ Indicated |
| **Cytotoxic** | Infarcts (dead tissue) → malignant cerebral edema; ischemic stroke, ICH, TBI | ❌ Contraindicated |

!!! warning "Corticosteroid Pearl"
    CCS are indicated for **vasogenic** edema (brain tumors, BBB disruption).
    CCS are **contraindicated** in cytotoxic edema (ischemic stroke, ICH, TBI).
